The story of "Burger & Fries" started about a decade ago. This fast-food chain's journey started with a high schooler dream of opening a fast-food chain among his favorite foods. Although, the high schooler wasn't expecting much from his ideas. Even then his dream soon became a reality after almost a decade of hardwork. Over 2000 "Burger & Fries" chains have opened across the United States alone, with a couple hundred over the world. We are devoted to serving you our best dishes and keep your expectations high.
